## Sidebar (from screenshot navigation/02_search_bar.png)

### Logo Area
- **"bloomerang"** in dark lowercase text with green leaf icon above the "b"
- **"CRM"** below in dark text with a **solid green bar** to the right
- White background

### Collapse Button
- White circle with "<" chevron, positioned at top-right of sidebar
- Collapses sidebar to icon-only mode

### Menu Items (exact order, top to bottom):
1. **Home** — green house icon (filled), **GREEN ACTIVE STATE** (light green bg ~#d7f5c3, green text, green left border)
2. **Constituents** — two-person icon (gray)
3. **Groups** — hexagon/honeycomb cluster icon (gray)
4. **Reports** — half-circle/pie chart icon (gray)
5. **Communications** — speech bubble icon (gray) + **dropdown chevron ▾** (has sub-menu)
6. **Data Tools** — folder/briefcase icon (gray) + **dropdown chevron ▾** (has sub-menu)
7. **Settings** — gear/cog icon (gray) + **dropdown chevron ▾** (has sub-menu)

### Styling Details
- White background
- Each item: icon (gray, ~20px) + label text (gray, ~14px, regular weight)
- Active item: light green background, green text, green icon
- Vertical spacing: ~48-52px per item
- Font: Clean sans-serif (appears to be the system/product font)
- Subtle separator lines between some items

### Sub-menu Items (from help center structure, not visible in collapsed view)
- **Communications** likely contains: Emails, Letters, Tasks, Interactions
- **Data Tools** likely contains: Import, Export, Merge, Custom Fields
- **Settings** likely contains: Organization, Users, Permissions, Integrations

### NOT visible in sidebar (may be accessed other ways):
- Transactions/Donations (likely under Constituents or a separate view)
- Campaigns (likely accessed through Reports or a dedicated section)
- Journey Automation (likely under Communications or its own section)
- Online Forms (likely under Communications)
- Memberships
- Payments
