- **Screenshots found:** No specific screenshot images identified in article
- **UI elements described:**
  - Proprietary formula developed by Dr. Adrian Sargeant
  - **Five engagement levels:** Cold, Cool, Warm, Hot, On Fire
  - Displayed on constituent Summary tab
  - **Positive engagement factors:**
    - Donations, pledges, consecutive-year payments
    - Current membership status (rated "Hot" or higher)
    - Year-over-year donation increases
    - In-person interactions (Special Events, Volunteer Activities)
    - Constituent-initiated interactions
    - Website visits

    - Survey responses
  - **Negative engagement factors:**
    - Lapsed donor status
    - Year-over-year donation decreases
    - Email unsubscriptions

## Generosity Score
- **URL:** https://help.bloomerang.com/en/articles/13929282-about-the-generosity-score
- **Screenshots found:** Referenced but not directly extractable from page structure
- **UI elements described:**
  - Free feature for all Bloomerang CRM customers
  - Displays as "a color-coded temperature meter" on constituent profiles
  - **Score levels:**
    - **On Fire** (DS1-1): Gifts of $5,000+ to nonprofits
    - **Hot** (DS1-2): Exceptional wealth markers including $2M+ real estate
    - **Warm** (DS1-3): Notable wealth markers including $1-2M real estate
    - **Cool** (DS1-4, DS1-5): Significant markers like $500K-1M properties
    - **Cold** (DS2, DS3): Minimal matches to giving or wealth indicators
    - **Not Scanned**: Constituents not yet screened
  - Powered by DonorSearch partnership (wealth prospecting tool with public wealth info)
  - Requires: first name, last name, U.S. address
  - **Location in UI:** Constituent account > Summary tab > Generosity tile
  - **Actions:** "View Generosity Details" link, "View in DonorSearch" link (if subscribed)
  - **Updates:** New constituents within 24 hours; existing every 6 months
  - **Reporting:** Can create reports with Generosity filter and Generosity column

## Sustainability Scorecard Basics
- **URL:** https://help.bloomerang.com/en/articles/12632888-sustainability-scorecard-basics
- **Screenshots found:** Referenced but URLs not directly extracted
- **UI elements described:**
  - Accessed via: Home > Sustainability Scorecard tile > "View Now" button
  - Assessment completed quarterly based on previous fiscal quarter data
  - Results plotted on graph showing fiscal year averages
  - **Color coding:** Green (doing well), Yellow (on path), Orange (needs work), Red (not sustainable)
  - **Metrics tracked:**
    - Face-to-Face Meetings count vs prior year
    - In-Kind Gifts count vs prior year
    - Monthly Donor Value (revenue / donor count / months to date)
    - Retention Rate (current vs prior year)
  - **Donor Pyramid Levels:**
    - Major: Over $2,500 total OR 8+ consecutive years
    - Mid: $500.01-$2,500 OR 4-7 consecutive years
    - Small: $500 or less OR 1-3 consecutive years

## Email Builder: Use the AI Content Assistant

- **Screenshots found:** Referenced but not directly extractable
- **UI elements described:**
  - Works with Title, Paragraph, List, and Button content blocks only
  - **"Write with AI" button** - appears on compatible content blocks
  - No data sent to AI unless "Write with AI" is clicked
  - **Two workflows:**

# Summary of Key UI Patterns Found

## Navigation Structure
- Left sidebar navigation (expandable/collapsible)
- Top search bar on every page
- Profile icon (first letter of name) top-right
- Notification bell icon
- Help icon
- Chat icon (lower left)
- Apps switcher icon

## Color Coding System
- **Engagement/Generosity levels:** Cold (blue/gray), Cool (light blue), Warm (yellow/orange), Hot (orange/red), On Fire (red/fire)
- **Sustainability Scorecard:** Green (good), Yellow (on path), Orange (needs work), Red (not sustainable)
- **Donor Retention Wheel:** Green/yellow/red based on retention percentage
- **Notifications:** Red dot for new notifications

## Common UI Patterns
- **Dropdown menus** on constituent records for quick actions (New Donation, Pledge, etc.)
- **"Save And" split buttons** with multiple save destinations
- **Action menus** on list items (View, Edit, Delete, Export)
- **Filter panels** with multiple category groups
- **Toggle switches** for features (Analytics, Penny AI, etc.)
- **Drag-and-drop** in email builder and automation priority
- **Tab-based navigation** on constituent records (Summary, Profile, Timeline, Relationships)
- **"Ask AI" button** in toolbar for Penny AI access
- **"Write with AI" button** on email content blocks

Amount Raised | Donor Retention | Donor Calls | Month Over Month Revenue | Campaigns | Tasks | Recent Constituents | Recent Reports | Sustainability Scorecard
