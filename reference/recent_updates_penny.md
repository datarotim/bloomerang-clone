## 1. Penny AI (Currently in BETA)

### UI Location & Access
- **"Ask AI" button** in the toolbar on **every page** in Bloomerang CRM
- Click button → Penny opens
- Always available, on every page

### Interface Design
- **Panel type:** Right-side slide-out chat panel
- **Interaction model:**
  - Select from suggested prompts OR type your own question
  - Start new conversations anytime
  - Read or continue past conversations
  - Penny remembers context within a conversation
  - No need for "perfect prompts" — just ask naturally

### Capabilities
- Grounded in organization's own CRM data
- Informed by 2,000+ consulting wins from Bloomerang's fundraising consultants
- Identifies at-risk donors early
- Surfaces high-potential opportunities
- Improves data quality
- Prepares fundraisers for meetings
- Explains WHY she makes recommendations
- Not a generic AI — strategic partner built into the Giving Platform
- Data never used to train external AI models

### Key Success Story
A customer used Penny to create a fundraising plan, hit a $1M matching goal, raised $2.5M. That customer became extremely sticky.
